FP2P Platform Privacy Notice

What data do we use?

  • FP2P user profile: name, corporate email, company, role, business line/area.
  • Operational records linked to documents and metadata necessary for execution, support, document control, and traceability.
  • Access and security: minimal technical identifiers to manage authenticated sessions and prevent unauthorized use.

Purposes

  • Execution and administration of services/contracts.
  • Support, quality assurance, and traceability of operations recorded in FP2P.
  • System security and access control.
  • Legal and contractual compliance (client and authorities’ requirements).

Legal Bases

  • Compliance with legal/contractual obligations.
  • Legitimate interest in system security and prevention of misuse.

Retention

This data is stored while the browser session is active and valid.

Cookies and similar technologies

  • FP2P uses only strictly necessary technologies to initiate and maintain authenticated sessions and preserve system security.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising cookies in FP2P.
  • If non-essential measurement tools are incorporated in the future, consent will be requested and this Notice will be updated.

Updates

We will publish any changes to this Notice, indicating the date of the last update.
